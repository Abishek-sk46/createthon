In Italy, must-visit places include the Colosseum, Leaning Tower of Pisa, Venice Canals, and Vatican City. Famous foods to try are Pizza, Pasta, Gelato, and Tiramisu. Popular restaurants include Osteria Francescana in Modena, Da Enzo al 29 in Rome, and La Pergola in Rome.

Suitable Price

A suitable price for tourists in another country depends on the destination, season, and type of travel. Budget travelers can expect to spend less on accommodation and food, while luxury tourists may pay more for high-end services and experiences.
